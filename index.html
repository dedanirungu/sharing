Fighting poverty through resources sharing

Machines that can be shared.

Chaff cutter

Chaff Cutter can assist the community develop animal feed storage and manage to overcome dry season

Interlocking brick making machine

Interlocking brick machine can change the houses from building with clay to CEB and hence improve the environment and hygiene

Diesel Powered Water pump

It can assist community irrigate farms during dry season and increase farm produce. But with Fueling cost

Solar Powered Water pump

It can assist community irrigate farms during dry season and increase farm produce. At no extra cost

Battery powered Welding set

Can assist community build windows, doors and other structure without dependancy of electricity

Maize cob remover

Can assist remove maize cob at high speed and hence improve farm production

Hand Pushed Tractors

Can assist community automate farming and hence improve farm production

Generator

Can assist community manage other farm activities that need heavy electricity need

Means of Transport Available

All of the above machines weight between 100kg to 250kg and can be transported by following ways.

  • Small Pull Cart
  • Donkey
  • Motorbike
  • Cargo TukTuk

Pull Cart

Pull cart are easy to build and can manage to move up to 150kg with ease.

Donkey

1 in every 15 family own's a donkey. It can move cargo upto 300kg.

Motorbike

1 in every 10 family own's a motorbike. It can move upto 100kg and can also mount a pull cart as a trailer.

Cargo TukTuk

In every village there is atleast 1 Three wheel cargo vehicle. It can move upto 600kg.
